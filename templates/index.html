DuellingDynasty helps you create and manage the fencing tournament of your dreams.

Easily organize and manage FENCING TOURNAMENTS

DuellingDynasty makes it easy to manage big and small fencing tournaments with different rulesets, including (multiple) preliminary groups, repechage and placement matches. It tracks scores and calculates standings and results in real-time. Save time and focus on what matters most - the competition.

How it works

DuellingDynasty is a Python-based application that hosts a webserver in a local (wifi) network. This way, it is possible for everyone to see current standings and matches from their mobile devices. It just needs one person as a host with a computer.
