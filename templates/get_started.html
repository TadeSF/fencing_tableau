Startlist

The Startlist file is a comma delimited CSV file, which contains all necessary and optional information about a Fencer. It must meet very strict formal requirements, such as very specific headers and alpha-3 Country Codes for the Nationality of the Fencer. We have explained everything about the Startlist CSV in our Userguide. You can also use our very easy Startlist-Builder or download the Template- or Example-File with the buttons below.

Fencing Pistes

Please specify how many fencing-pistes are available at your tournament. Usually (with the Piste-Assignment-Mode "Algorithmic"), the pistes are assigned dynamically to the matches of each round.
If you don't want that, there are two other optoins: In the Preliminary Rounds, the mode "Semi-Auto" assigns each group their individual piste. After that during Elimination, it works as described before. In "Manual" mode on the other hand, each piste must be assigned manually as suggested.

Elimination Mode

Please specify which Elimination Mode the tournament shoud have. You have three options: KO, Repechage and Placements. In KO, fencers compete in a single-elimination tournament until only one fencer remains. In Repechage, fencers who lose early in the tournament are given a second chance to compete in a separate bracket. KO with Placement Matches is a combination of the previous two modes, where fencers who lose early in the tournament still have the chance to compete for their final tournament ranking. In each mode, all matchmaking is automatic.
